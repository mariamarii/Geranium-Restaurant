Private Events

HOST YOUR
EVENT AT Geranium!

Enjoy the warm, intimate atmosphere of the Writer’s Room and escape to Rome by way of the West Village with group dining and private events at HAMF. Group dining is available for parties of 8 or more. Parties of up to 30 guests may be seated in the private Writer’s Room, an intimate, banquette-lined space decorated in warm earth tones. Geranium is also available for full restaurant events.

THE PRIVATE DINING ROOM

Geranium's Private Dining Room is an elegant and intimate space that can seat up to 22 people. The centerpiece of the room is a single, stately table crafted by Maine artisan Greg Lipton. Set beneath a wood-beamed ceiling and rustic chandeliers by Celeste Gainey, the table is complemented by early American antiques and art adorning the walls. Floral arrangements by Roberta Bendavid are available.

The room has a capacity of 22 guests.


THE GARDEN

Join us in The Garden, a reimagined outdoor space by Chef Mike Anthony and the team at Gramercy Tavern. Whether it’s for midday drinks or a cocktail reception before your seated dinner in our Private Dining Room, The Garden is a great place to enjoy summer’s bounty alongside our seasonal cocktail and wine program.

We can accommodate up to 50 guests.


THE PRIVATE DINING ROOM

Now available for private events, our Vault Room is the perfect place for business dinners and celebratory gatherings. Named for its soaring ceilings and designed to feel both spacious and inviting, our semi-private option sits within our main Dining Room to provide guests with the classic Geranium experience featuring Chef Mike Anthony’s award-winning seasonal tasting menu.

We can accommodate up to 40 guests.


FULL RESTAURANT BUYOUT

Geranium is available for full restaurant buyouts. Our team will help make your celebration truly special in our beautiful, historic space.

We can accommodate up to 120 guests for a seated event.
